Welcome to MSU SECON 2015

IEEE SouthEastCon is the annual IEEE Region 3 Technical, Professional, and Student Conference. It brings together Computer Science, Electrical and Computer Engineering professions, faculty and students to share the latest information through technical sessions, tutorials and exhibits. It is the most influential conference in Region 3 for promoting awareness of the technical contributions made by our profession to the advancement of engineering science and to the community.
