Competition Overview

The theme of SECON 2015 is road tripping. Competitors must design a robot that can navigate a course and play four commonly found games on road trips. The robot starts upon a start light, and when the light turns of the round begins. The robot must follow a white line to four different boxes and in each box there is a game. The robot must play Simon for fifteen seconds correctly, draw "IEEE" on an Etch-a-Sketch, twist one row of a Rubiks Cube 180°, and pick up exactly one card from a deck of cards. After all of the games have been completed the robot must then cross a finish line. These tasks must be completed in under five minutes and they must be completed autonomously.
